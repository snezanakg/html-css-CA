About Us

Rainy Days is an outerwear lifestyle brand. Its collections blend a conceptual-meets-functional design approach, a strong urban inspiration, and a signature fabric identity. A coated waterproof fabric palette inspired by Rainy Days' first design - a contemporary reinterpretation of classic rubber jackets. Rainy Days brings its neo-Scandinavian aesthetic, unisex collections, and consistently surprising curation to consumers across the world.
