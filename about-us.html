About RainyDays

RainyDays is an outerwear lifestyle brand. Our collections blend functional design with a strong urban inspiration. Our first design was a contemporary reinterpretation of classic rubber jackets, setting the foundation for our waterproof fabric identity.

We are dedicated to providing high-quality, stylish, and functional outerwear for everyone, ensuring protection against harsh weather while maintaining elegance.
